Research Fellowship Programs

Supporting outstanding researchers who demonstrate excellence in innovation and academic leadership.

Program Objectives

  • Promote high-quality and ethical research.
  • Support innovative and interdisciplinary research projects.
  • Encourage international collaboration.
  • Enhance research publication in reputed indexed journals.
  • Build future academic leaders.

Fellowship Benefits

  • Monthly stipend (as per ARP guidelines).
  • Research publication support.
  • Editorial mentorship and guidance.
  • Access to ARP academic network.
  • Certificate of Fellowship.
  • Opportunity to serve as reviewer/editorial member.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Hold a Ph.D. degree from a recognized university
  • Have strong academic and research background
  • Demonstrate research publications in reputed journals
  • Submit a clear and innovative research proposal
  • Obtain consent from a host institution/supervisor

Duration

Range from 6 months to 2 years, depending on the research proposal and funding approval.

Research Areas

Engineering & Technology Computer Science & AI Life Sciences & Biotechnology Management & Social Sciences Physical Sciences Interdisciplinary Research

Application & Selection

Follow these steps to apply for the ARP Research Fellowship Program.

1

Document Submission

Submit your Updated CV, Research Proposal (5–10 pages), Publication List, Recommendation Letter, and Acceptance letter from Host Institution via email.

2

Initial Screening

The Academic Committee performs an initial screening of all submitted documents.

3

Proposal Review

A rigorous review of the research proposal is conducted by subject matter experts.

4

Interview

Selected candidates may be invited for an online or offline interview.

5

Final Approval

Final approval is granted by the ARP Fellowship Board.
